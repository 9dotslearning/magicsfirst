At magiCSfirst, you'll learn about creating art with computer science. You'll make interactive stories, animated drawings, digital sounds, and more. And to make that art you'll explore yet another art — the captivating art called coding.

Written especially for beginners and particularly for tweens and teens, our lessons focus on learning by tinkering. They offer concrete examples and take small steps, with plenty of time for experimentation and lots of room for invention. Most importantly, in each lesson there's a little magic first, like this.

Our blog unspools thoughts about teaching computer science, with some successes and many more questions than answers.
