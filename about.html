About

The proprietor of magiCSfirst is Terry Dash. She did straight time as an information technology manager for 25 years, with the last 10 years happily managing systems for an elementary school. This was a lucky break, since she had long since, in a hopeful and youthful moment, acquired a masters degree in education from Harvard that was otherwise completely useless. At the elementary school, she rescued a precious few moments each week from ravenous hardware and software systems to teach students in grades 2 through 9 about digital citizenship, troubleshooting, typing, and — most joyfully — about coding.

And then she retired. But in a completely unexpected twist of fate, her local school system almost simultaneously launched a middle school computer science program. Since teaching computer science is the most joyful thing she knows, she unretired. For the next 8 years, she taught Scratch, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Twine, and robotics, and she hung out with a lively cohort of tweens.

magiCSfirst is the distillation of what those students taught her. In the best learning, the magic's first. After students feel the magic, they teach you what they need to know.
