About

The proprietor of magiCSfirst is Terry Dash. She did 25 years as an information technology manager, with the last 10 years at an elementary school. This was a lucky break, since she had long since, in a hopeful and youthful moment, acquired a masters degree in education from Harvard that was otherwise completely useless. At the elementary school, she rescued a little time each week from imperious hardware and software systems to teach students in grades 2 through 9 about digital citizenship, troubleshooting, typing, and — most joyfully — about coding.

And then she retired. But in a coincident twist of fate, her local school system launched a middle school computer science program. Since teaching computer science was the most joyful thing she knew, she unretired. For the next 8 years, she taught Scratch, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Twine, and robotics, and she hung out with a lively cohort of tweens in a classroom of her own.

magiCSfirst is the distillation of what those students taught her: in the best learning, the magic's first. When students find the magic, they teach you what they need to know.
